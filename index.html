Minting a Crack Cat is super simple. Connect your Solana wallet and mint.
If you don't have a wallet do the following steps:
  1. Download a Solana wallet extension (Phantom, Solfare and Sollet recommended) for your browser (Google Chrome recommended).
  2. Buy Solana (SOL) on an exchange like FTX, Binance, Coinbase etc. and send it to your wallet public address.
  3. Connect your wallet to our website by clicking on the "Connect" button. (Button will be displayed when pre-sale starts)
  4. Click the Mint button on https://crackcats.io and approve the transaction in your wallet.
Mobile devices (not recommended):
  1. Create a new wallet or import an existing one on Sollet.io and send some SOL as described in this video: Sollet.io tutorial.
  2. Open CrackCats.io in a new tab and connect the created wallet by clicking "Connect Wallet" > "Sollet"
